# Taxometer Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 13, 2026

Taxometer calculates trip distance and fare on the user's device. The app does not require an account and does not operate an application backend.

## Data used by the app

During an active ride, Taxometer uses precise location, timestamps, speed, and accuracy information to calculate distance, waiting time, and fare. The app also stores tariff settings, completed-trip summaries, and receipt information locally on the device.

Taxometer does not transmit this information to the developer, an advertising network, or an analytics service. Data processed only on the device is not collected by the developer.

## Background location

If the user grants Always location access, Taxometer may access location in the background only while a ride is active. This allows calculation to continue while the screen is locked. Tracking is stopped when the ride is paused or finished. The user can revoke location access in iOS Settings at any time.

## Sharing and deletion

The app shares a PDF receipt only after the user taps the share button and chooses a destination in the system share sheet. The selected destination's privacy terms then apply. Users can delete individual completed trips inside the app. Removing the app also removes its locally stored data, subject to the operating system's backup behavior.

## Contact

Privacy questions can be sent to the support address published with Taxometer in App Store Connect.
